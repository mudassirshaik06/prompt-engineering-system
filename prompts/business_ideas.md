# Business Ideas Prompt

## ❌ Bad Prompt
Give me some business ideas.

## ✅ Optimized Prompt
You are an expert business strategist.

Context:
The user is a BTech student looking for small-scale profitable projects in India.

Instructions:
- Suggest 5–7 actionable ideas
- Include initial investment range
- Highlight potential ROI
- Mention legal & easy-to-start options
- Format in bullet points
